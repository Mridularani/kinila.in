How we work

Innovations only matter when they improve real lives

This is the belief that drives every engagement. Kinila Life Sciences brings together deep scientific expertise and a systems-level understanding of healthcare delivery — with the conviction that knowledge must translate into practice to have any value.

We work with biotech, pharma, and healthcare organisations that want rigorous thinking, honest counsel, and a partner who understands both the science and the system it operates within.

01

Evidence first

Every recommendation is grounded in data, literature, and real-world experience — not generic frameworks.

02

Systems thinking

We look beyond the immediate problem to understand the system it sits in — regulatory, clinical, organisational, and cultural.

03

Equity by design

Solutions that only work for well-resourced settings are incomplete. We design with underserved populations in mind from the start.

04

Translational focus

From research to regulatory submission to real-world implementation — we stay with the work until it lands in practice.

What we offer

Services & consulting areas

Research institutions are built to generate knowledge — not to navigate funding ecosystems. Kinila Life Sciences bridges that gap, offering hands-on support across the full arc from strategy to submission to implementation.

Grant writing & proposal development

Securing funding requires a different skill set from doing the science. We work with academic and research partners to develop compelling, well-structured grant proposals — from funder identification and narrative strategy to completing online applications, budget justification, and submission compliance. We take the administrative burden off researchers so they can focus on the work.

Learn more about grant strategy →
Research strategy & implementation

Translating research into practice — whether a clinical intervention, a community health programme, or a technology deployment — requires planning that most research teams are not resourced to do. We contribute to implementation design, stakeholder alignment, and evaluation frameworks from the earliest stages of a project.

Academic & industry partnerships

We connect research institutions with industry partners, NGOs, healthcare systems, and funders through our networks across global health, biotechnology, and healthcare innovation. Whether you are looking for a co-investigator, an industry collaborator, or a delivery partner, we help build the right table.

Regulatory & clinical development

Strategic guidance on regulatory submissions (FDA, EMA, ICMR), clinical trial design, diagnostic development, and navigating complex multi-stakeholder approval processes across India and internationally.

Real-world data & evidence generation

From study design to analysis, we support evidence generation from real-world healthcare data — including outcomes research, post-market studies, and data strategies for grant applications and regulatory submissions.

Building AI healthcare for the last mile

The consulting practice is only one part of the work. Kinila is also building an AI health platform designed for the 3.5 billion people who lack access to basic healthcare — starting with the clinical decision support tool already deployed in coastal Karnataka.

Unlike existing solutions designed for hospitals, our tools work on smartphones in low-resource settings, designed for community health workers at the front line of care.
